# NPCs, Factions & Characters

## The Adventurer's Guilds

The backbone of dungeon exploration. Each major town maintains its own guild chapter with a Guildmaster. They maintain bounties, handle treasure appraisals, and ensure adventurers have access to supplies before descents.

### Guildmasters

- **Buchanan Chapter** — *Master Aldwyn* — A grizzled veteran who lost his left arm in a dungeon collapse. Despite his disability, he runs the most efficient guild in the region.
- **Benton Chapter** — *Vera the Sharp* — A former adventurer turned administrator. Known for her strict inventory tracking and zero-tolerance policy on theft.
- **Coloma Chapter** — *Thom Blackhammer* — A dwarf seer with a reputation for reading fortune before expeditions. Expensive, but his predictions have saved lives.

## The Church of the Sundered Flame

A religious organization that believes The Sundering was a divine test of humanity's worth. They operate temples in major towns and believe that dungeon delving is spiritual work.

### Notable Members

- **High Priestess Miriam** — Head of the Buchanan temple. Offers blessings to adventurers (mostly morale boosting, but the faithful swear by them)
- **Brother Aldric** — A scholar priest researching the connection between The Sundering and the abyss

## The Deepmarket Collective

A loose network of merchants, smugglers, and traders who deal in dungeon goods. They're price-setters and fences for rare artifacts recovered from the depths.

### Key Figures

- **Maltus the Appraiser** — Runs a shop in Buchanan. Can identify any artifact for a fee.
- **The Widow** — An enigmatic black-market dealer. No one knows her real name, but she brokers the most dangerous trades.

## Scholars & Researchers

### The Codex Society

An academic faction dedicated to deciphering the secrets of the Elder Civilization. They operate from a converted vault chamber in Niles and seek rare tomes and inscriptions.

- **Master Elira** — Head researcher, obsessed with finding proof of Elder Civilization magic

### Arcanists' Consortium

A smaller, more secretive faction of mages. They believe dangerous knowledge should be carefully controlled. Relations with the Codex Society are... tense.

- **Archon Vestis** — Leads from the shadows, rarely seen in public

## Independent NPCs

### Captain Foss (Buchanan Garrison)

Oversees town guard and militia. Neutral on dungeon delving but concerned about adventurers who attract monsters back to town.

### Nyssa the Hedge

A retired adventurer who now runs a small tavern near the Buchanan dungeon entrance. She hears all the gossip and sometimes trades information for coin or drinks.

## Mysterious Figures

### The Keeper

Rumors speak of an ancient entity that dwells in the deepest vault chambers. It's said to guard something of immense power or danger. No explorer who has encountered it has lived to provide details.

### The Whisperer

An unconfirmed figure that appears in tavern stories. Some claim they're a ghost, others that they're a rogue AI from the Elder Civilization attempting to communicate with moderns.
