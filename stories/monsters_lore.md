# Bestiary: Monsters of the Dungeon

## Origins

Most creatures encountered in the Dungeon of Doom fall into three categories:

1. **Abyssal Entities** — Creatures from beyond reality, drawn through The Sundering. These are often intelligent and malicious.
2. **Corrupted Ancients** — Once-natural creatures twisted by abyssal energies. They retain some animal intelligence but are far more dangerous.
3. **Vault Guardians** — Magical constructs created by the Elder Civilization. Many have degraded over time; some remain dangerous defenders of deep chambers.

## Common Threats

### Goblin Scramblers (Tier 1 - Weak)

Once human-sized creatures, these have been twisted into hunched, clawed beings. They're weak individually but hunt in packs. Scavengers by nature, they collect shiny objects obsessively.

**Lore:** Originally, these were corrupted by exposure to abyssal mist in the upper vault chambers. They've since bred true, creating entire colonies.

### Shadow Serpents (Tier 2 - Moderate)

Long, sinuous creatures of living shadow. They can unhinge their jaws to swallow adventurers whole. Semi-intelligent and territorial.

**Lore:** Believed to be native to the Abyss, they thrive in the deepest, darkest chambers. Some scholars think they're scouts for something larger.

### Stone Golems (Tier 2-3 - Moderate to Dangerous)

Humanoid constructs of ancient stone, animated by Elder magic. Nearly immune to physical damage. Slow but relentless.

**Lore:** These are confirmed Vault Guardians. Their magic is still potent after millennia. Destroying one often releases trapped knowledge (quest items, ancient scrolls).

### Flame Wraiths (Tier 3 - Dangerous)

Beings of pure fire and will, they float above the ground and incinerate everything in their path. Difficult to harm with conventional weapons.

**Lore:** Mystery surrounds their origin. Some think they're sentient expressions of abyssal fire. Others believe they're corrupted souls.

### The Devourer Worms (Tier 4 - Deadly)

Massive, burrowing creatures that can swallow a horse. They emerge from walls and floors unexpectedly. A single encounter can end a dungeon run.

**Lore:** Only encountered in the deepest dungeon levels. Possibly a natural species that has simply grown massive in the magical environment. Or something brought from the Abyss at the moment of The Sundering.

## Boss Creatures

### The Vault's Sentinel (Legendary)

A colossal construct that guards the entrance to the deepest vault chambers. Made of stone, brass, and magical runes, it is considered nearly invincible. Only a handful of adventurers claim to have defeated it.

**Lore:** Created by Elder Civilization engineers as a failsafe. It has endured for millennia and grows more sentient with each passing century.

## Special Mechanics

### Abyssal Corruption

Some monsters have a special property: defeating them may corrupt nearby adventurers or items with abyssal energy. This can provide temporary power boosts but risks going mad.

### Regeneration

Creatures tied to the Abyss sometimes regenerate if not destroyed thoroughly. Fire, magic, or holy relics are often required to ensure permanent death.
