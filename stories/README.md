## World Map (Meta)

The dungeon connects multiple towns across a region. Each town has:
- A unique character and faction alignment
- Its own guild chapter
- Specific quest hooks and mysteries
- Connections to deeper dungeon floors

## Themes

The Dungeon of Doom explores:
- **Knowledge vs. Secrecy** — Who should know the truth about The Sundering?
- **Progress vs. Caution** — How far into the deep is safe?
- **Community vs. Power** — Do we explore together or compete for resources?
- **The Unknown** — What awaits in the deepest levels?
- **Agency** — Different factions offer different paths; which do you choose?
