# Timeline of Events

## Ancient Era (Dates Unknown)

- **Year 0** — The Elder Civilization flourishes across the known lands
- **Year 0 + 2000** — The Grand Vaults are constructed as repositories of knowledge and artifacts
- **Year 0 + 4500** — First signs of planar instability detected by the Mage Councils

## The Sundering (Year -300)

- **The Sundering** — A catastrophic magical rupture tears through reality. The barrier between worlds collapses. Abyssal entities pour forth.
- **-300** — Surface civilizations collapse within months
- **-298** — Survivors seal themselves in the Grand Vaults for protection
- **-280** — The Vault Wars begin as monsters breach the lower seals

## The Refuge Era (-280 to -100)

- **-200** — First generation of vault-born are fully adapted to underground life
- **-180** — Ancient scrolls are deciphered; vault dwellers learn the location of deeper treasures
- **-120** — Expeditions begin to map the upper vault levels; first successful treasure recovered

## The Emergence Era (-100 to Present)

- **-100** — The Great Ascent: survivors breach to the surface, establishing the first surface settlements
- **-80** — Towns of Buchanan, Benton, and Coloma are founded as dungeon outposts
- **-40** — The Adventurer's Guilds form across major towns
- **-5** — A mysterious artifact is discovered in the Buchanan Quest Vault; its purpose remains unknown
- **Year 1 (Present)** — The Age of Delving. Adventurers explore the dungeons in earnest, seeking fortune and renown.

## Looming Shadows

Scholars whisper of tremors deep beneath the earth. Some say the deepest seals are weakening again. Others claim something beneath the dungeons is waking up.
