# Background of Dungeon Crawler of Doom

## The World of Eldoria

In the ancient realm of Eldoria, where the veil between the mortal world and the ethereal planes grows thin, a shadow has fallen over the land. Centuries ago, the great sorcerer-king Arcanoth delved too deep into forbidden magics, awakening primordial evils that now fester in the depths of endless dungeons. These labyrinthine caverns, scattered across the overworld, are not mere holes in the ground—they are gateways to nightmares, filled with monstrous guardians, cursed treasures, and forgotten secrets.

Eldoria is a vast, procedurally woven tapestry of terrains: lush grasslands where travelers once roamed freely, dense forests hiding ancient ruins, towering mountains that pierce the sky, and serene waters that mask underwater horrors. Towns dot the landscape, serving as havens for weary adventurers, bustling with merchants, blacksmiths, and mystics. But beneath this facade of normalcy lies a world on the brink of chaos.

## The Curse of the Dungeons

The dungeons themselves are alive with malevolent intent. Some are natural formations, twisted by dark energies; others were crafted by long-extinct civilizations as prisons for beings too terrible to destroy. Wandering monsters roam these halls, driven by an insatiable hunger for the living. Treasure chests gleam with promise, but many contain traps or curses that bind the soul. Fountains offer respite—restoring health or mana—but some are poisoned wells that drain the life force of the unwary.

Players who enter these depths do so at great peril. Death is not the end; in the normal mode, fallen heroes respawn in overworld towns, their gold diminished but their spirit unbroken. In hardcore mode, true death awaits, erasing their legacy forever. Yet, for those who survive, glory beckons: leveling up grants power, spells unlock arcane might, and alliances forged in co-op combat can turn the tide against overwhelming odds.

## The Adventurers' Call

You are one such adventurer—a Fighter wielding brute strength, a Mage commanding elemental fury, a Thief slipping through shadows, or a Cleric channeling divine light. Alone or with allies, you venture forth to slay beasts, claim riches, and uncover quests that reveal the greater threats looming over Eldoria. Rumors speak of a grand conspiracy, where the dungeons are not random but part of a larger ritual to summon an elder god.

Will you be the hero who breaks the curse, or will you join the legions lost to the abyss? The choice—and the adventure—awaits in Dungeon Crawler of Doom, a retro multiplayer RPG where every crawl could be your last... or your legend's beginning.
