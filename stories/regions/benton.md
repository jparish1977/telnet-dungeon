# Benton: The Harbor

Benton developed as a strategic outpost along the coastal waters. While Buchanan is the commercial hub, Benton serves as the primary supplier of rare herbs, medicinal plants, and sea-based provisions to all dungeon towns.

## Geography

Built on a natural harbor with deep-water access, Benton is surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills. The **Benton Cavern** dungeon entrance is half-submerged, accessible only during low tide — this unique feature made it one of the most mysterious entrances to discover.

**Key Locations:**
- **The Tide Gate** — The half-submerged dungeon entrance
- **Vera's Guild House** — Where Vera the Sharp runs the Benton chapter with military precision
- **The Herbalist's Quarter** — Where rare plants are cultivated, dried, and traded
- **Smuggler's Dock** — A less legitimate trade hub for under-the-table deals
- **The Lighthouse** — An actual lighthouse converted into an adventurer's rest stop with observation decks

## History & Culture

### A Different Philosophy

While Buchanan grew as a trade and military hub, Benton developed its own character: pragmatism mixed with discretion. The guild here is smaller but every member is rigorously vetted.

### The Herbalist's Pact

A historic agreement between Benton's herb-growers and the dungeon creatures. It's said that certain creatures in the Benton Cavern leave the herbalist foragers alone in exchange for safe passage through upper levels. No one can verify this, but the Benton herbalists trade far safer than those from other towns.

## Present Day

Benton has a smaller population than Buchanan but higher per-capita wealth. The atmosphere is more secretive, more cautious. Vera the Sharp ensures excellent discipline in her guild chapter.

## Rumors & Hooks

- A shipment of rare healing herbs disappeared from the docks. Vera suspects internal theft but is offering good coin to anyone who can track them.
- The Smuggler's Dock has a job that requires discretion — moving something "sensitive" without alerting the guild.
- A cartographer in Benton claims to have mapped the Benton Cavern more thoroughly than anyone. Their maps are locked away, but adventurers with the right coin might access them.
- Whispers suggest that something ancient sleeps beneath Benton's harbor. Harbor workers occasionally find strange, warm stones in the water.

## Unique Mechanics

**Tidal Gating** — The Tide Gate is only accessible during specific tidal windows. This limits dungeon runs but also creates bottlenecks where creatures can be trapped or ambushed.

**Herbalist Buffs** — Potions from Benton herbalists are more potent than standard fare. Adventurers buy at a premium despite the higher prices.
