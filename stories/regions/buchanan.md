# Buchanan: The Gateway

Buchanan is the largest and most established of the dungeon towns. Its central dungeon entrance — known as the **Buchanan Rift** — was the first major breakthrough to the surface during The Emergence.

## Geography

Buchanan sits at the convergence of two rivers and was chosen as the first settlement because of its natural defenses and proximity to fresh water. The town has grown to accommodate thousands of adventurers, merchants, and scholars.

**Key Locations:**
- **The Rift** — The primary dungeon entrance. A massive crack in the earth from The Sundering.
- **Guild Hall** — Headquarters of the Buchanan Adventurer's Guild. Master Aldwyn presides over all operations.
- **The Broken Anvil** — Tavern and meeting place for adventurers.
- **The Arcanist's Tower** — Where the Arcanists' Consortium operates in secret.
- **Temple of the Flame** — Church of the Sundered Flame's main chapel.

## History

### The First Ascent (-100)

A group of vault dwellers broke through to daylight in what is now Buchanan. They found a thriving ecosystem above and a friendly mixed population living in makeshift settlements. Together, they built the first permanent town.

### The Boom (-80 to -40)

Buchanan became the center of dungeon commerce. Treasure flowed upward, finished goods flowed downward. The town's population exploded from hundreds to thousands.

### The Incident of -32

A group of adventurers delved too deep and opened a seal they shouldn't have. A swarm of creatures poured into the upper chambers. The panic nearly destroyed the town. New protocols were established to prevent unsanctioned deep exploration.

## Present Day

Buchanan is divided into several districts:

- **The Trade Quarter** — Markets, merchant stalls, appraisers
- **The Adventurer's Quarter** — Taverns, equipment shops, guild offices
- **The Academic Quarter** — Scholar halls, libraries, research centers
- **The Residential Quarter** — Where townsfolk live away from the chaos

## Rumors & Hooks

- Master Aldwyn is rumored to be seeking a rare artifact lost during The Incident. High reward for its retrieval.
- The Widow (black-market dealer) is looking for someone discreet to retrieve a stolen ledger from the Guild Hall.
- A scholar from the Codex Society believes something precious is buried beneath the old temple foundations.
- Nyssa the Hedge has been asking adventurers about missing townsfolk with an unusual intensity.

## Unique Challenges

**The Rift Winds** — A phenomenon where magical energy discharged from the dungeon creates hot, disorienting winds in Buchanan proper. Worse on days when many adventurers are delving deep.

**Rift Sickness** — Prolonged exposure to the Rift's magical aura can cause minor hallucinations and paranoia. Most adventurers recover after a day's rest above ground.
