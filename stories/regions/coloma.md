# Coloma: The Quest Vault

Coloma is the smallest of the major towns but carries the most historical significance. The Coloma Quest Vault is where the Elder Civilization stored their most important records, artifacts, and knowledge — and where adventurers now seek legendary treasures.

## Geography

Nestled in a forested valley, Coloma is picturesque but isolated. The town exists primarily to support exploration of the Coloma Vaults and access to the historic sites. It has a frontier feel compared to Buchanan's established commerce.

**Key Locations:**
- **The Grand Vault Entrance** — A massive stone archway carved with Elder runes
- **Thom's Fortune Hall** — Where Thom Blackhammer reads fortunes and manages the local guild
- **The Scholar's Archive** — A collaborative library run by the Codex Society and local historians
- **The Watching Stone** — An ancient monument overlooking the valley; its purpose is unknown
- **The Artifact Repository** — A secure vault where recovered artifacts are held pending authentication

## History & Significance

### The Last Settlement

Coloma was the last of the three original towns to be established, but it quickly became the most prestigious. Adventurers who wanted to claim true legendary status came here.

### The Discovery of -15

A team of scholars and adventurers discovered a series of unbroken seals within the Coloma Quest Vault. Inside: perfectly preserved artifacts, functioning mechanical devices, and crystal tablets with data still intact. This single discovery revolutionized understanding of the Elder Civilization's capabilities.

### Present-Day Scholarship

Today, Coloma is considered the academic heart of dungeon exploration. The Codex Society maintains its second-largest chapter here. Debates rage about what to explore, what to preserve, and what should remain sealed forever.

## Present Day

Coloma attracts a different crowd than Buchanan. Fewer mercenaries, more idealists. Thom Blackhammer's fortune readings are sought because he has uncanny accuracy — adventurers plan entire careers around his predictions.

## Rumors & Hooks

- A fortune-teller's prediction says that three adventurers will uncover something that "changes everything" in the Coloma Vaults. Everyone wants to know who Thom was talking about.
- The Scholar's Archive is looking for someone to retrieve a stolen research journal from a previous expedition. The information inside is sought by multiple factions.
- An artifact stored in the Repository is said to be malfunctioning. Its original purpose is unknown. The first adventurer to explain what it does will earn the gratitude of the scholarly community.
- Thom has gone quiet about his fortunes lately. When pressed, he mutters about "the deep calling" and "old things waking."

## Unique Mechanics

**Destiny Fortunes** — Thom's readings provide semi-randomized bonuses/warnings on entry to the vault. Players who ask for a reading get unique quest hooks or stat adjustments.

**Artifact Unlocking** — Recovered artifacts from the vault can be brought to the Archive for analysis. This provides lore and can unlock recipes, new abilities, or quests.
